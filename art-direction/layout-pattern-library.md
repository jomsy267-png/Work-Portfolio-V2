## Starting patterns

### Cinematic Hero + Editorial Scroll

Best for:
Projects with one strong visual asset.

Structure:
- full-screen hero image or video
- minimal project metadata
- quiet intro text
- large image sequence
- detail grid
- final full-bleed payoff

Why it works:
Creates instant perceived value and lets visuals carry the story.

When not to use:
Projects with weak imagery or projects needing technical explanation.

### Swiss Case Study Grid

Best for:
Systems, signage, production, UI, or structured projects.

Structure:
- strong title grid
- metadata column
- technical/process section
- aligned image modules
- detail crops
- final system overview

Why it works:
Makes complex work feel intentional and organized.

### Gallery With Rhythm Breaks

Best for:
Projects with many strong images.

Structure:
- large hero
- 2-column image pair
- text break
- full-bleed image
- asymmetrical grid
- final image

Why it works:
Prevents the page from becoming a boring image dump.
