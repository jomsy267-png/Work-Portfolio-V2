# Final Art Director Review Prompt

Review the redesigned project page like a senior art director.

Check:

1. Does it preserve the existing website brand?
2. Is the hero strong enough?
3. Is the image sequence intentional?
4. Does the page have rhythm?
5. Is the typography controlled?
6. Is there enough negative space?
7. Does motion improve the page?
8. Does anything feel generic?
9. Does anything feel AI-generated?
10. Would this hold up beside a premium New York agency portfolio?

Then provide:

- Pass/fail
- Specific issues
- Exact improvements
- Files to adjust
- Final recommendation
